Recovery Journey

Early Symptoms

Persistent swelling below both ankles and two painful ulcers on the left ankle that would not heal.

Diagnosis

A Doppler examination confirmed advanced varicose vein disease requiring surgical treatment.

Surgery

Vein surgery was performed and post-operative wound management began.

Dressing Changes

Regular dressing sessions were carried out during the recovery phase.

Vacuum Dressing Therapy

Vacuum dressing therapy was initiated to encourage wound healing and tissue recovery.

Current Status

Treatment remains ongoing. Further treatment decisions, including possible grafting, will depend on healing progress and specialist review.

To Be Continued...

This recovery journey is still in progress. Future updates will document additional milestones, treatment decisions and healing progress.
